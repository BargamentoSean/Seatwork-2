Bullmastiff Dog Breed

The Bullmastiff dog breed is a muscular dog who have crosses between the mastiff-type dogs and the bull-baiting dogs.

The documented history of the bullmastiff traces to the late 1800s. Gamekeepers for large estates in England needed a courageous dog to catch and subdue poachers on their land.
The dog was to creep up silently and subdue, but not maul, the trespasser. The cross between a mastiff and bulldog provided a dog that combined the speed and strength to do the job.

The Bullmastiff is a loyal and protective companion which is safe for children to interact, and is devoted to their own master. While stubborn and drooly, it is worth for the situations that calls for it.
